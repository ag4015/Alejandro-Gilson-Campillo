About me

I'm Alejandro Gilson Campillo. I designed this site so you can get to know me on a personal level. My career, my portfolio, my hobbies and much more.

I am specialized in the development of software for demanding problems that require efficient solutions. I have a broad experience in the area of signal processing, machine learning and general purpose software.
