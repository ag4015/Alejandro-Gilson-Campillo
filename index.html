About me

I'm Alejandro Gilson Campillo. I designed this site so you can get to know me on a personal level. My career, my portfolio, my hobbies and much more.

I am a student at Imperial College London (ICL) studying an MEng in Electrical and Electronic Engineering. I was born in Madrid but graduated from secondary school in Barcelona. I am specialized in the development of software for demanding problems that require efficient solutions. I have a broad experience in the area of signal processing, machine learning and general purpose software.

If, after all, I happen to have caught your interest, don't hesitate to contact me for any opportunities, ideas or just a coffee and a chat. For more details, my CV is available for download.
